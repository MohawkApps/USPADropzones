We are open May-Sept, 5 days a week, closed Tuesdays and Wednesdays. During the Fall and Spring we are open weekends. Our facilities consist of snack bar, refreshments, indoor packing area and outdoor lawn. We are a C-182 DZ with occasional helicopter and turbine aircraft. We specialize in first time tandem skydives and advanced flying skills. We have on sight Instructors Examiners for Tandem rating, Coach rating and Static Line rating.
