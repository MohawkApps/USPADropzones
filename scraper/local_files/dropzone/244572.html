Tobati Aventuras Aereas

  TAA, Tobati, Paraguay

Batalla de Acosta Ñu 3250
Cordillera 
Tobati Paraguay

3 km E of Tobati
3 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivetobati.com.py
 
  skydivetobati@gmail.com
  open map
 
  1 c-182, 1 C-206, 1 CASA

Tobati Adventures is available all year round. Normally, operating a Cessna 206. There is camping available on the dropzone. The dropzone is located near the city of Tobati. To get to the dropzone from Asuncion, take route 2 towards Civdad del este until you get to Caarupe, then take the detour towards Tobati. Once in Tobati you will pass the town church. Take the second right and go 3 km. The dropzone will be on the left.
