It's a startup drop zone, open 7 days a week during season (10 months a year). It's a 5 hour drive from Bengaluru International Airport, 4 hours from Hyerabad International Airport. Rigs of all sizes available. We have an experienced USPA AFF instructor who is also S/L-I, IAD-I and TI (Strong and Sigma). We permit all kinds of jumps subject to BSRs of USPA and insurance requirements of India.
