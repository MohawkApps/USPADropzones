Skydive Elsinore

  Skylark Airport (CA89), Elsinore, CA

20701 Cereal St
Lake Elsinore CA 92530-9650 US

90 miles SE of Los Angeles
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveelsinore.com
  (951) 245-9939
  skydive@skydiveelsinore.com
  open map
  Western Region
  2 Cessna 208 Caravan, 1 Super Twin Otter

Open year round! One hour from San Diego and Los Angeles. Nestled in the scenic recreational valley of Lake Elsinore, CA. Beautiful lake and ocean views nestled in the surrounding San Bernadino and Ortega Mountains. FS, FF and WS organizing and coaching available. Covered packing and creeping areas. Known as one of the worlds friendliest drop-zone. Please feel free to contact us so that we can assist you in planning your stay.

Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses Canopy Course

  09/21/2019 - 09/22/2019

  Skydive Elsinore   Lake Elsinore California

   Minos, John, Course Director
John Minos, Main Contact

 John@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

John Minos is teaching Flight 101 & 102 at Skydive Elsinore on the 9th & 10th of November 2019. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 9 November Flight 102 - Sunday 10 November To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3938 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3939
