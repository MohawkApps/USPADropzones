The Jumping Place

  Statesboro-Bulloch County Airport (KTBR), Statesboro, GA

127 Airport Dr
Statesboro GA 30461-7565 US

35 miles W of Savannah International Airport
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.thejumpingplace.com
  (912) 322-7282
  thejumpingplace@gmail.com
  open map
  Southeast Region
  2 Cessna 182s

Currently, open every weekend. Will open during the week if you have 4 jumpers willing to commit to 8 jumps or more. From Savannah(I95), take 16 West to exit 127. At exit 127, go NW on State Rd 67 for 10.8 miles. Turn right on US 301 bypass for 4.5 miles. Turn right onto Airport Blvd. In 1/2 mile, turn right onto Airport Dr. We are in the large hangar on your left. Blue hangar with large The Jumping Place sign. Large hangar with 900 acres to land on! Call 912-322-7282 for the Gate Access Code.
