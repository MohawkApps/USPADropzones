Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses

  02/15/2020 - 02/16/2020

  Skydive DeLand, Inc.   Deland Florida

   Klockner, Alex G., Course Director
Alex Klockner, Main Contact

 alex@lfight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Alex Klockner will be teaching Flight 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive DeLand on the 15th & 16th of February 2020. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. Flight 101 - Saturday 15 February Flight 102 - Sunday 16 February To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4212 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4213
