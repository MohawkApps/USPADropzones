Flight-1 201 & 101 Canopy Courses at Cleveland Skydiving Center

  05/30/2020 - 05/31/2020

  Cleveland Skydiving Center   Garrettsville Ohio

   Stocum, Aaron M., Course Director
Aaron Stocum, Main Contact

 Aaron@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Aaron Stocum will be teaching Flight-1 201 & 101 Canopy Courses at Cleveland Skydiving Center on the 30th & 31st of May 2020. Module 200 provides a safe environment for skydivers wanting to progress their canopy skills to the next level in the area of high performance landings. **REQUIREMENT TO TAKE FLIGHT 201: Minimum of 200 jumps** We also highly recommend to have completed & attended Flight 101 and 102. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 201 - Saturday 30 May Flight 101 - Sunday 31 May Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. To join, sign up here: 201 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4150 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4149
