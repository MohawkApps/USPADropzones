Triple Crown Event 1—30-Way FS

  06/13/2020 - 06/14/2020

  Skydive Suffolk   Suffolk VA

   Katie, Main Contact

 757-474-5346

 events@skydivesuffolk.com

 http://www.skydivesuffolk.com

This is the first event of its kind in Virginia where in one season we will attempt three different state records. On three different weekends throughout the season, we will attempt a multi-aircraft state record for 30-way sequential FS (Belly), 30-way head-down, and 30-way head-up. If all three records are achieved, any participant that successfully participated in all three will be awarded the 2020 Triple Crown and get their name permanently placed on a banner in the hangar along with a few other prizes. This will be an annual event we hope to grow and make it larger and more challenging each year. Become a part of history in Virginia and get some records in the books! To be considered, please send a resume to Ross at dzm@skydivesuffolk.com Triple Crown Dates: June 13th- Weather day 14th (RW) July-11th- Weather day 12th (HD) August 22nd- Weather day 23rd (HU)

Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses

  10/10/2020 - 10/11/2020

  Skydive Suffolk   Suffolk Virginia

   Kaz Sheekey, Main Contact

 Kaz@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Kaz Sheekey will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive Suffolk on the 10th & 11th of October 2020. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 10 October Flight 102 - Sunday 11 October To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4300 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4302
