Miami Skydiving Center

  Tamiami Airport (TMB), Miami, FL

14005 SW 127th St
Miami FL 33186-5338 US

15 miles miles   S of Miami Airport
0.1 mil to nearest hotel


  http://www.MiamiSkydivingCenter.com
  (305) 235-3483
  info@miamiskydivingcenter.com
  open map
  Southeast Region
  1 Cessna 182

Located at the Miami Executive Airport, Miami Skydiving Center is the closest skydiving center to Miami. Only a short drive from South beach, Miami Skydiving Center offers amazing views of the Miami skyline, Biscayne Bay, the Florida Keys, and much more on every jump. Miami Skydiving Center specializes in tandem skydiving for first time jumpers.
