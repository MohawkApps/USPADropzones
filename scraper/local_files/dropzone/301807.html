Skydive Qatar

  Um Al Shokot, Al Khor, Qatar

Al Shamal Road Exit 44
Al Khor - Qatar

50 km miles   S of Al Khor
15 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveqatar.com
  974 4032 9173
  info@skydiveqatar.com
  open map
  Foreign
  Cessna 208 Grand Caravan EX

Open 6 days a week, October to May. Take Al Shamal Rd north from Doha to Exit 44. Awesome views, new aircraft and equipment, air conditioned packing, cafes, camping and kids area. Drop zone facilities in true desert-style luxury! Coaching available in FS, AE, CF, CP and Wingsuiting.
