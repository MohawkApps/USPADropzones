Skydive Qatar

  Um Al Shokot, Al Khor, Qatar

Al Shamal Road Exit 44
Al Khor - Qatar

44 km N of Doha
15 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveqatar.com
  974 4032 9173
  info@skydiveqatar.com
  open map
  Foreign
  1 Cessna 208 Grand Caravan EX

Open 6 days a week, October to May. Take Al Shamal Rd north from Doha to Exit 44. Awesome views, new aircraft and equipment, air conditioned packing, cafe, rest and kids area. Drop zone setup in true desert-style luxury! Coaching available in FS, AE, CF, CP and Wingsuiting.
