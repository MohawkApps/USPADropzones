Skydive Portugal is an experienced drop zone and school dedicated to professional and fun skydiving. Open year-round and strategically located in Evora-the UNESCO capital of Alentejo. Located only 50 min away from Lisbon by car, 220km from Faro, and 400km from Oporto. Skydive Portugal is already the reference for skydivers all around the world! From seasonal boogies to special events, at Skydive Portugal all disciplines are welcome. Instruction and coaching are available from our highly trained and friendly staff.

Night Jumps

  02/07/2020 - 02/08/2020

  Skydive Portugal   Évora Évora, Portugal

   Filipe Oliveira, Main Contact

 +351912929981

 filipe@skydiveportugal.pt

 https://skydiveportugal.pt/saltos-noturnos/

Skydive Portugal is planning night jumps for February 7th and 8th, depending on the weather conditions. These jumps are mandatory for the D License requirements (2 jumps). To participate in these jumps, you must: - Have a B license or superior; - Have jumped recently in Skydive Portugal Dropzone (or previously do 2 familiarization jumps at the day of the event or during the previous day); - Be present at the night jump’s briefing – on February 7th, 5:00pm; - Have an altimeter that can light up. Skydive Portugal will provide: - Your body lights (front and back); - The dropzone illumination, easy to identify and obstacle free landing area; - Rental gear available (if you have a 120ft or smaller, and want to jump with a bigger canopy). Prices: - Slot at 12000ft: €50 (lights included) - Equipment rental + packing: €25 15-WAY NIGHT FORMATION We will also hold a 15-way Night Formation. To participate in the formation, you must meet the following requirements: - Have previously performed night jumps; - Have participated in formations of 15-way or higher; - Participate successfully in the trainings for the formation, on sunday the 2nd February, at Skydive Portugal. BOOK YOUR SLOT AT: https://skydiveportugal.pt/saltos-noturnos/
