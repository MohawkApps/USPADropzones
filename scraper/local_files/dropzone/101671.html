Skydive Empuriabrava

  Empuriabrava Airport, Empuriabrava, Spain

Sector Aeroclub S/N
Empuriabrava 17487 Spain

150 km miles   N of Barcelona
1 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveempuriabrava.com
  34(972)450111
  info@skydiveempuriabrava.com
  open map
  Foreign
  1 Beech 99, 1 Super Twin Otter, 1 Pilatus Porter

Open seven days a week all year. Situated 1.5 hours north of Barcelona on the Costa Brava. Just 2km from the Mediterranean Sea in Empuriabrava with many hotels, apartments, and restaurants within walking distance. Wind Tunnel and Team Training facilities on site. Coaching and training from basic to advanced levels offered. Note: All skydivers are required to use AADs.

Flight - 1 101 Canopy Course at Skydive Empuriabrava Canopy Course

  11/03/2018 - 11/03/2018

  Skydive Empuriabrava   Cataluna Spain

   Pete Allum, Main Contact

 Pete@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

World medal winning FS and CP champion Pete Allum will be teaching Flight 101 Canopy Course at Skydive Empuriabrava on the 3rd of November 2018. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 3 November To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3384
