Sky's The Limit

  Pocono Stroudsburg Airport (N53), East Stroudsburg, PA

124 Airstrip Rd
East Stroudsburg PA 18301-8578 US

75 miles miles   W of New York City
0.1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skysthelimit.net
  (800)335-JUMP
  teresa@skysthelimit.net
  open map
  Eastern Region
  1 Cessna 182, 1 Cessna 208, 1 Twin Otter

Open year round, closest skydiving center to New York City-only 75 miles west. Located near the beautiful Pocono Mountains with views of the Manhattan Skyline and the Delaware water gap. Facilities include 9,000 square foot heated hangar, indoor and outdoor covered packing, professional packers, indoor bathroom and shower facilities, the World's Fastest Grand Caravan and turbine aircraft to 13,500ft. RW and Advanced Freefly (AFP) coaching available. Organizing every weekend. Team rates available.
