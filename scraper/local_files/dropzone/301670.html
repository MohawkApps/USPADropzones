Dallas Skydive Center

  Caddo Mills Municipal Airport (7F3), Caddo Mills, TX

3517 County Road 2615
Caddo Mills TX 75135-7510 US

36 miles E of Dallas
7 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.dallasskydivecenter.com
  (972) 552-7790
  info@dallasskydivecenter.com
  open map

The closest DZ to Dallas! We have a massive landing area, large open hanger and comfy viewing area! See the Dallas Skyline from up to 13,500ft while enjoying the small drop zone feel. We're a good vibe DZ that welcomes all disciplines. Shut Up and Jump!
