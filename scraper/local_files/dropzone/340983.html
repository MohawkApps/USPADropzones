Skydive Saar (FSZ - SAAR)

  Saarlouis-Dueren, Saarlouis, Germany




10 km E of Saarlouis
.5 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydive-saar.de
  004906837-74136
  info@fsz-saar.de
  open map
 
 

Full service DZ that hosts numerous boogies and events. DZ has large landing areas, multiple carpeted indoor areas, creeper area, roof top chill area, excellent cafe, a paved runway and a grand and baby supervan. DZ is located close to Luxemburg IAP and has many hotel and restaurants options.
