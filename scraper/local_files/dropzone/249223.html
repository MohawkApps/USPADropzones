TNT-Brothers Dropzone

  Clinceni, Clinceni, Romania

Aerodrom Clinceni, Str. Aeroportului
nr. 114 
Cornetu, Ilfov County Romania

18 km SW of Bucharest
1.5 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.tnt-brothers.ro
  40-724-695867
  office@tnt-brothers.ro
  open map
 
  1 Pilatus Porter

Open April to November, Friday to Sunday; Thursday in summer months. Air conditioned bar with snacks, beverages and warm food. Hangar with packing area and heated video, course and rigger rooms as well as a large room to hang out and debrief. Coaching available for intermediate formation skydiving, freefly, performance canopy piloting and accuracy.
