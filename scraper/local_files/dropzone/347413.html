Skydive Aros

  Johannisbergs Flygplats, Stockholm Vasteras, Sweden

Johannisberg 11
Vasteras 72591 Sweden

112 km W of Stockholm
2 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.fkaros.se
  46 21121666
  info@fkaros.se
  open map
  Foreign
 

We are one of the biggest skydiving clubs in Sweden. Open weekends April to the end of October and seven days a week during summer months. Tandems and fun jumping. Free Bunkhouse, kitchen, cafe, bar sauna and indoor packing area. Full gear shop and rigger loft 2 km from DZ. Easy transport with train from Stockholm Central or air shuttle from Arlanda or Stockholm, Vasteras Airport. Requirements: Valid FAI license and insurance.
