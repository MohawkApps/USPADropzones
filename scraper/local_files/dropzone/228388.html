Skydive East Tennessee

  Dumplin Field Airport (86TN), Dandridge, TN

1542 W Dumplin Valley Rd
Dandridge TN 37725-5615 US

22 miles miles   E of Knoxville
3.7 to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveET.com
  (865) 223-5155
  info@skydiveet.com
  open map
  Southern Region
  1 Cessna 182, 1 Cessna 206

Private 84-acre airpark, full-time commercial drop zone. Closest jump center to Knoxville, Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg, and Smoky Mountains National Park. USPA designated training center for Tandem, AFF and Static Line. Swoop pond, shower facilities, camping on DZ, gear rental, climate controlled indoor packing, training room, video room, wireless internet, packers, freefall camera flyers for hire. Less than two miles off of Interstate 40 at Exit 412, follow blue interstate signage to DZ entrance on W Dumplin Valley Rd.
