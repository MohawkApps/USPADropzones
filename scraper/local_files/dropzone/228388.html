Skydive East Tennessee

  Dumplin Field Airport (86TN), Dandridge, TN

1542 W Dumplin Valley Rd
Dandridge TN 37725-5615 US

22 miles E of Knoxville
3.7 to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveET.com
  (865) 223-5155
  setmanifest@gmail.com
  open map
  Southern Region
  1 Cessna 182

Private 84-acre airpark, full-time commercial drop zone. Closest jump center to Knoxville, Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg, and Smoky Mountains National Park. USPA designated training center for Tandem, AFF and Static Line. Shower facilities, camping on DZ, RV sites with water & electricity and tent camping.
