Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses Official USPA Event Canopy Course

  09/28/2019 - 09/29/2019

  Aerohio Skydiving Center   Rittman Ohio

   Aaron Stocum, Course Director
Aaron Stocum, Main Contact

 Aaron@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Aaron Stocum will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 canopy courses at Aerohio Skydiving Center on the 28th & 29th of September 2019. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 28 September Flight 102 - Sunday 29 September Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3841 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3842
