Skydive Phoenix is located just 12 miles south of downtown Phoenix. Open 7 days a week year round. We pride ourselves in making your experience safe, fun, and memorable. We welcome all levels of jumpers, from first-timers to the experienced. We offer multiple disciplines of instruction, specializing in tandem student training and AFF/ASP.
