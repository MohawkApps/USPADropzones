1110 Airport Rd #577
Overton NV 89040 US

60 miles miles   NE of Las Vegas
2 miles to nearest hotel

Skydive Fyrosity is open 7 days a week 365 days a year! Located just off of I-15 about 60 miles NE of Las Vegas, NV. Take Exit 93 toward Overton and follow signs to Overton Airport. Air conditioned packing, pool table, FREE Wi-Fi, and lockers available. Full-service RV park only 1 mile from the airport.
