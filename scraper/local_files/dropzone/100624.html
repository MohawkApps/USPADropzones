Des Moines Skydivers

  Winterset Municipal Airport (3Y3), Winterset, IA

3405 E 8th St
Des Moines IA 50316-1145 US

20 miles SW of Des Moines
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.desmoinesskydivers.com
  (515) 243-1711
  manager@desmoinesskydivers.com
  open map
  Central Region
  1 Cessna 182

Only 15 minutes from I-35 and I-80. Open weekends and some weekdays--call the dropzone for jumping information. Full facilities including showers, A/C and heated hangar, gear rental and student as well as experienced jumper training. Only club owned and operated DZ in Iowa. Turbine aircraft available June-September. Home of the Couch Freaks.
