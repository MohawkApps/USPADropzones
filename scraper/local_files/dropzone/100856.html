Flight - 1 101 & 102 Courses at Chicagoland Skydiving Center Canopy Course

  09/28/2019 - 09/29/2019

  Chicagoland Skydiving Center   Rochelle IL

   Kaz Sheekey, Course Director
Chicagoland Skydiving Center, Main Contact

 customercare@skydivecsc.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Kaz Sheekey will be teaching Flight 101 & 102 canopy courses at Chicagoland Skydiving Center on the 28th & 29th of September 2019. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 28 September Flight 102 - Sunday 29 September To join, sign up here: 101 - http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3753 102 - http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3754
