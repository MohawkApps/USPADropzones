2333 Delray Rd
Thomaston GA 30286-1425 US

50 miles S of Atlanta
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveatlanta.com

Open Friday, Saturday & Sunday year-round. Full-service DZ. Located just off I-75, one hour from Atlanta, Macon, and Columbus. Large, open landing areas.
