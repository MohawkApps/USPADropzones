Irish Parachute Club, Ltd.

  Clonbullogue Airfield, Edenderry, Ireland

Clonbullogue Airfield
Edenderry 
Co Offaly - Ireland

64 km SW of Dublin
13 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydive.ie

  info@skydive.ie
  open map
  Foreign
  1 Cessna 182, 1 PAC 750

The Irish Parachute Club is over 50 years in operation. We do approximately 10,000 jumps annually. We run static line, tandem and AFF courses every weekend. There are about 160 regular members. Located 64 km from our capital city, Dublin, which is an hour drive from the DZ. Our club is open every weekend all year round.
