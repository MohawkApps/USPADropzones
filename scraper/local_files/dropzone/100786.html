Madera Parachute Center

  Madera Municipal Airport (MAE), Madera, CA

4130 Aviation Dr Ste D
Madera CA 93637-9442 US

13 miles miles   N of Fresno
0.5 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivemadera.com
  (559) 674-1278
  skydivemadera@gmail.com
  open map
  Pacific Region
  1 Cessna 182

Open Saturday and Sunday. Huge on-airport landing area and grass or hangar packing areas. 10,500-ft. altitude with great view of Sierra Nevada and San Joaquin Valley. Friendly, unpretentious, family atmosphere. Activities for non-jumpers close by (golf, theater and shopping). All skill levels welcome.
