Skydive Diani

  Ukunda Airstrip, Ukunda, Kenya

Diani Beach Rd
Diani Kenya

36 km miles   S of Mombasa
10 meters to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivediani.com
  254 701 300 400
  jump@skydivediani.com

Open 7 days a week all year round. Voted by Yahoo as one of the most scenic places to skydive in the world! The drop zone is located right next to the Indian Ocean. We offer a restaurant, bar, packing area, and a dirt diving area with creepers. Sunset BBQ's & pool are available.
