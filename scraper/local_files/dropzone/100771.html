1401 Airport Rd
Boulder City NV 89005-3675 US

20 miles SE of Las Vegas
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivelasvegas.com
  (702) SKY-DIVE
  jump@skydivelasvegas.com

Welcome to Las Vegas' premier student skydiving center specializing in student tandem training. We fly a P-750 XSTOL, utilize the newest state-of-the-art equipment, and offer complimentary transportation from the Las Vegas Strip in our passenger vans. Enjoy spectacular views of Hoover Dam and Lake Mead as your friends and family watch you land right next to our facility. Your experience at Skydive Las Vegas will be second-to-none! Open 7 days a week 365 days a year. Visit us on Facebook at www.facebook.com/skydivelasvegas
