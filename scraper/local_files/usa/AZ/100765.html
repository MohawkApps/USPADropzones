Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive Arizona Canopy Course

  11/10/2018 - 11/11/2018

  Skydive Arizona   Eloy Arizona

   Justin Price, Main Contact

 Justinp@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

PD Factory Team member, Justin Price will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive Arizona on the 10th & 11th of November 2018. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 10 November Flight 102 - Sunday 11 November Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3403 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3404
