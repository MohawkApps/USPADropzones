Welcome to Skydive Oregon. Come join us at our private airport that is dedicated solely to skydiving! Enjoy our fast 850 HP turbine aircraft throughout the jump season as well as 65-acres of grass landing area, an air-conditioned/heated clubhouse, shaded packing decks, wireless internet, adjacent camping area and spectacular mountain views. Nearby swimming, kayaking, skiing and climbing.
