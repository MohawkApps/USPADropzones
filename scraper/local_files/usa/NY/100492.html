Frontier Skydivers

  Hollands International Airport (85N), Newfane, NY

3316 Beebe Rd
Newfane NY 14108-9657 US

15 miles miles   NE of Niagara Falls
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.frontierskydivers.com
  (716) 751-6170
  manifest@frontierskydivers.com
  open map
  Eastern Region
  1 Cessna 182

Frontier Skydivers, one of Western New York's oldest sport parachute clubs, is located in Newfane, NY. The dropzone is located 15 miles from Niagara Falls, 20 miles from Buffalo and St. Catherines and 50 miles from Rochester. Also within driving distance of Toronto, Hamilton and Erie, PA.
